TDS Page Designs

The Client

TDS Telecommunications LLC (TDS Telecom/TDS®) delivers high-speed internet, TV entertainment, and phone services to a mix of small to mid-sized urban, suburban and rural communities throughout the U.S. With 1.2 million connections, TDS is a rapidly growing technology company. Powered by fiber-optics and new industry-leading technologies, TDS delivers up to 8 Gigabit internet speeds and offers internet-protocol based TV entertainment solutions along with traditional phone services. TDS also offers businesses VoIP advanced communications solutions, dedicated internet service, data networking, and hosted-managed services.

TDS Business—Internet Product Section

General design updates for the internet product section on TDS Telecom's business/commercial market. These were done to modernize the presentation of this section, as well as encourage more cohesion among the pages.

TDS Fiber—Business Section Redesign

A redesign was requested for the business section in summer of 2023. This redesign was done to:

  • Align the design of the business section homepage with the overall/residential homepage
  • Make the informational/product pages more visually appealing with the use of iconography and imagery
  • Modernize the design of the section overall

TDS Fiber—Homepage Redesign

In 2021, a request was made to redesign the homepage, with the largest reason being to create trust in the client. I suggested we add an overall navigation to the website, including the hompage, as well as create a hero section for main links and large imagery. Since then, iterations and revisions have been made, including the most recent addition of an introductory FAQ section, added in 2024.
